Luvox Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Luvox is highly effective in reducing symptoms of OCD, anxiety, and panic attacks. Several users describe it as "life-saving" and express gratitude for its impact on their daily functioning. One patient stated, "Luvox has been great in treating my Bipolar depression... It's a very good drug in the treatment of depression with little or no side effects." Others appreciate the decreased obsessive thoughts and an increased ability to engage in normal life activities. Users often mention feeling more like themselves again after starting this medication.

Negative

Common complaints about Luvox include weight gain, insomnia, fatigue, and sexual dysfunction. Several users noted severe side effects, including profound tiredness, dizziness, and a feeling of being "zombie-like." One user expressed frustration, saying, "The side effects were intense and not worth it." Reports of worsening symptoms like increased panic or depression after dosage adjustments are also present, as is a fear of withdrawal symptoms when discontinuing the medication.

Noteworthy

One user shared a dramatic account of experiencing severe convulsions and a rapid heartbeat requiring emergency care after just a few days on Luvox. Another mentioned losing their voice and needing Botox injections, stating, "I can no longer sing... had to resign from teaching due to loss of voice."

Conclusion

Overall, experiences with Luvox are varied, with significant positive outcomes for some patients and debilitating side effects for others. While it may be a miracle drug for many, careful consideration and monitoring of side effects are crucial for those embarking on this treatment.
