Valproic Acid Oral

Positive

Many patients report positive effects of Valproic Acid, particularly its effectiveness in stabilizing mood and controlling manic episodes associated with bipolar disorder. A long-term user noted, "when taken properly seizures have ceased," while another shared, “it has helped my daughter very much.” Some users also described significant improvements in their chronic migraines, citing almost complete relief: "I started taking this, and I have only had 1 migraine since."

Negative

Common negative experiences include weight gain, gastrointestinal issues, and mental fog. One patient mentioned extreme side effects leading to "uncontrollable feet movements" and memory issues, expressing significant distress: "I would not recommend this medication for anything." Several others pointed out weight gain and hunger as problematic, stating it was challenging to lose the additional pounds: "I have gained over 50 pounds and 12 dress sizes in less than 10 months."

Noteworthy

A few unique experiences stand out. One patient reported severe adverse effects, including personality changes and depression in their son, stating, "I don't like this drug." Another shared a dramatic metamorphosis, stating, "I was almost comatose!" from an excessive initial dosage.

Conclusion

Overall, Valproic Acid has been beneficial for many in managing mood stability and seizures, yet substantial side effects, particularly related to weight and gastrointestinal distress, have raised concerns among users. Attention to dosage and monitoring of side effects seems crucial for optimizing its use.
