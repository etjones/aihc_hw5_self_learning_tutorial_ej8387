Paroxetine Oral

Positive

Many patients have reported significant improvements in their anxiety and mood after starting Paroxetine. Users frequently note that it helps reduce panic attacks and stabilizes emotional states. One user said, "Having been taking 20MG Paroxetine... I definitely notice improvement with severe emotional highs and lows." Several patients stated that the medication allowed them to regain control, improve social interactions, and feel more like their old selves. Some found that their OCD symptoms lessened and they became more active in daily life.

Negative

Common negative experiences include significant weight gain, sexual side effects, and withdrawal symptoms when discontinuing the medication. One individual expressed frustration, stating, "It definitely improved my mood, until I gained 60 lbs in a year." Others eloquently described their struggle to taper off, reporting "severe dizziness, headaches, brain zapping" during this process. Side effects such as increased perspiration, lethargy, and difficulties with urination were also mentioned.

Noteworthy

  • A patient experienced life-changing withdrawal symptoms: "The following 6 days... were the worst in my life; extreme nausea, can't sleep, severe anxiety... I wish I had never taken this med."
  • Another user reported developing psychotic symptoms after increasing their dose, leading to hospitalization, and advised extreme caution regarding the medication's use.

Conclusion

Paroxetine has had a mixed reception among users; while many find substantial improvements in anxiety and mood, the accompanying side effects can be troubling, particularly concerning weight gain and sexual dysfunction. Withdrawal symptoms can also be severe for some, and users are generally advised to approach dosage adjustments carefully. Overall, while it is a lifeline for many, it's essential to weigh the benefits against potential risks.
