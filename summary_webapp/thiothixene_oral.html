Thiothixene Oral

Positive

Patients generally highlight that Thiothixene has been effective in managing symptoms of schizophrenia. One reviewer states, "it has helped with my schizophrenia... much more easy to deal with," emphasizing a significant improvement in their condition. Another individual reports that it’s the only medication that has successfully treated their sister’s disorder, leading to "many breakthroughs" after trying multiple alternatives.

Negative

Conversely, some users report severe adverse effects. One patient experienced "severe major suicidal depression after a few weeks," while another noted debilitating side effects, stating, "he feels it is causing him to bite his tongue while eating," which led to severe injuries. Some users found the medication ineffective, with one stating simply, "this med did little if anything for me."

Noteworthy

An unusual report comes from a user who mentioned that their brother experienced extreme tongue biting, which he attributed to the medication, stating, "he bites so hard his tongue would bleed."

Conclusion

Overall, Thiothixene seems to have a mixed reception, with significant positive effects for some, particularly in managing schizophrenia symptoms, while others report severe depression and ineffective treatment. The variation in experiences suggests that this medication may work well for some patients but can pose serious risks for others.
