Desipramine Oral

Positive

Many patients report significant improvements in mental health conditions such as PTSD, depression, and chronic pain, particularly for IBS and Fibromyalgia. One review states, "This medication has changed my life. No more diarrhea," while another patient emphasizes, "This drug has given me back my life again." Others mention feeling "less anxious" and achieving "the joy of living." Some found it effective where other antidepressants failed, with one stating it "literally saved my life."

Negative

Conversely, there are numerous reports of severe side effects, including debilitating constipation, increased depression, and heart-related issues. One patient described a harrowing experience: "I can barely walk or eat... my heart races," while another experienced intense side effects leading them to refer to desipramine as "this dreadful drug." Common complaints also mention dry mouth, fatigue, mood swings, and feelings of being drugged or emotionally flat.

Noteworthy

Several patients reported life-altering effects from the medication, both negative and positive. A user who had been on Desipramine for over 20 years declared it had "saved my life," while another noted how a small dose led to "terrible mood swings and uncontrollable anger," prompting a cessation of use.

Conclusion

Desipramine shows a polarizing range of effectiveness, helping many patients achieve symptom relief while causing severe adverse effects in others. Finding the right dosage appears crucial for success, and those considering the medication should be aware of both its potential benefits and risks.
