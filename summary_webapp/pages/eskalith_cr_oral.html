Eskalith CR Oral

Positive

Many patients report positive experiences with Eskalith CR, noting its effectiveness in managing symptoms related to bipolar disorder. One user highlighted, "I have taken many different medications over the last 8 years and this one has been the most effective by far." Another stated that it has successfully controlled their mania for 12 years. Additionally, some users mentioned that combining Eskalith with other medications, such as Zyprexa, yielded even better results.

Negative

On the downside, several patients expressed dissatisfaction, with one stating, "I see very little change in my behaviour," after a year on the medication. Others reported feelings of emotional numbness, with one stating they were "no longer capable of having genuine feelings," and expressed frustration about the limited advancements in medication for their condition.

Noteworthy

One patient shared a particularly troubling experience, stating "I wish there were another way besides drugs. I live through the drugs, and I'm not living at all." This highlights the struggle some users feel with the emotional toll of their treatment.

Conclusion

Overall, while Eskalith CR has been beneficial for many in managing bipolar disorder symptoms, others report it lacks efficacy and can lead to emotional numbness. The varied experiences underscore the complex nature of psychiatric medication and the need for individualized treatment approaches.
