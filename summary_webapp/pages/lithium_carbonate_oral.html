Lithium Carbonate Oral

Positive

Many patients reported significant mood stabilization and a reduction in manic symptoms after starting Lithium Carbonate. Some users described the medication as life-changing, with one stating, "THIS DRUG HAS BEEN MY LIFE SAVER." Others emphasized that it helped them regain control over their emotions and daily lives, making them feel “normal” for the first time in years. One patient noted that their suicidal thoughts have diminished significantly, attributing their new outlook on life to Lithium.

Negative

Commonly mentioned side effects included weight gain, hand tremors, increased thirst, and frequent urination. Many patients expressed frustration over weight gain, with some stating they gained significant amounts, such as 30-80 pounds. Serious concerns were raised about lithium toxicity and potential long-term health risks, including kidney damage and thyroid issues. As one reviewer put it, “Lithium is toxic. I now take N-Acetylcysteine...It destroyed my life."

Noteworthy

Some exceptional experiences were highlighted, such as one user who claimed to have attempted suicide multiple times before starting Lithium, which they credited for saving their life. Another patient described a terrifying experience that led to hospitalization due to lithium toxicity, stating, "I was practically in a coma... I will not ever take this medication again.”

Conclusion

Overall, patient experiences with Lithium Carbonate are mixed, with many appreciating its mood-stabilizing effects while grappling with unwanted side effects and serious health concerns. The sentiment suggests that while Lithium may offer life-changing benefits for some, careful monitoring and a personalized approach to treatment are vital for minimizing adverse effects.
