Aristada Intramuscular

Positive

Patients have expressed strong approval for Aristada Intramuscular, particularly praising its effectiveness in treating severe mental health issues. One user shared, "I've been on this medicine for over a year and it helped me a lot," highlighting its positive impact on their ability to manage daily life, including working and attending school. Another reviewer noted that it made their auditory hallucinations "more manageable," signifying improved quality of life.

Negative

On the downside, some users reported significant side effects, with weight gain being a common concern, as noted by a patient who mentioned gaining 20 lbs. Additionally, there are concerns about allergic reactions; one review mentioned serious reactions and suggested using Benadryl as a countermeasure.

Noteworthy

A standout review highlighted, "if there is anyone that is having a severe allergic reaction, and/or anaphylaxis... Benadryl may be of benefit," indicating that while the drug can be effective, it carries serious risks for some individuals.

Conclusion

Overall, patient experiences with Aristada Intramuscular are mixed. While many users find it life-changing and effective for managing severe symptoms, issues like weight gain and potential allergic reactions warrant careful consideration.
