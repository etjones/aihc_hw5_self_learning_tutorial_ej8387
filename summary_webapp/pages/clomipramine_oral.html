Clomipramine Oral

Positive

Many patients report significant improvements in their mental health after starting Clomipramine, particularly for conditions like OCD and severe anxiety. One user noted, "This drug saved my life. I feel more and more like myself each day." Another mentioned it helped them finally express buried emotions, highlighting its effectiveness in treating emotional issues. A common point of praise is its ability to manage obsessive thoughts, with several users stating a remarkable reduction in symptoms after weeks or months of use.

Negative

However, side effects are a recurring theme, with numerous patients experiencing issues like drowsiness, constipation, and dry mouth. One user stated, "The side effects were pretty intense at first," while another reported severe reactions, including heart palpitations and dizziness. Some individuals experienced mood instability or worsening symptoms, such as hypomania or anxiety, leading them to stop taking the medication.

Noteworthy

One standout review detailed a life-changing experience: "It didn't have immediate effect, but after 6 months... I am now functioning like a normal human being." In contrast, another patient described a terrifying reaction involving severe nausea and shaking after just one dose, leading them to seek hospitalization.

Conclusion

Overall, Clomipramine proves to be a potent medication for many, especially in treating OCD and anxiety, but it's essential for potential users to be aware of possible severe side effects. Each individual's response can vary significantly, making close monitoring necessary.
