Asenapine Sublingual

Positive

Many patients report that Asenapine is effective in alleviating symptoms of bipolar disorder and psychosis. Positive experiences often highlight its ability to significantly reduce anxiety and improve mood, making them feel more "alert" and capable of completing tasks. One user noted, "I feel like I woke up from a long sleep," reflecting a sense of rejuvenation. Several reviewers recommend the medication for its effectiveness despite some side effects.

Negative

Common negative experiences include significant tiredness, especially in the morning, and various side effects such as akathisia, tardive dyskinesia, and involuntary movements. One patient expressed frustration, stating, "I have found the side effects outweigh the benefits," indicating a struggle with the medication's impact on daily functioning and overall well-being.

Noteworthy

One review stands out with an extremely positive view, stating, "this treatment is awesome. I love drugs," which suggests a very strong, perhaps even euphoric, response to the medication, although it lacks detail.

Conclusion

Asenapine demonstrates a mix of strong positive effects on mood stabilization and symptom reduction for some patients, countered by complaints about tiredness and unpleasant side effects. The experiences highlight the importance of weighing benefits against drawbacks while managing expectations.
