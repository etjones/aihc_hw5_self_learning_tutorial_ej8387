Budeprion XL Oral

Positive

Some patients report significant improvements in mood and energy levels while taking Budeprion XL. Several users noted that it helped alleviate their depression and they felt more engaged in life. One patient stated, "This medication has changed my life. I had lost my smile and now I found it." Others mentioned successful weight loss and improved productivity, feeling that the medication gave them their lives back.

Negative

Many patients experienced severe side effects, including irritability, mood swings, depression worsening, and suicidal thoughts. A user described profound changes: "I became very angry and mean... I wanted to hurt myself and others." Others noted insomnia, weight gain, and physical symptoms like headaches and gastrointestinal issues. A common sentiment was frustration with being switched from Wellbutrin to Budeprion, with users claiming the latter did not work effectively.

Noteworthy

Several outlier reviews highlighted extreme adverse experiences, such as one patient who reported "visions of my undergarments" and a list of debilitating symptoms including slurred speech and excessive sweating. Another user expressed feeling "murderous" and experiencing chest pain after starting Budeprion, indicating a dangerous escalation in mood disturbances.

Conclusion

Overall, patient reviews of Budeprion XL reflect a highly mixed response. While some find relief from depression, many others report severe negative side effects, particularly anger, irritability, and worsening depression. The general consensus suggests caution, particularly regarding the switch from the brand name Wellbutrin to this generic form.
