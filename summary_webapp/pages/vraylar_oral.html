Vraylar Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Vraylar has significantly improved their mood and helped manage symptoms related to bipolar disorder, depression, and schizophrenia. For instance, one patient stated, "This medicine has changed my life. I had no idea the depression I was in for years. I really feel better." Another user mentioned, "Vraylar saved my life... I'm not irritable like I used to be and my husband has really seen a change in my life." Users also appreciated the medication's ability to alleviate symptoms like hallucinations and anxiety, leading to a more positive outlook on life.

Negative

Commonly reported side effects include significant weight gain, sedation, and anxiety. One user expressed, "It DOES make you gain ALOT of weight... I even tried to starve myself and still gained." Others reported experiencing severe agitation, akathisia, and increased anxiety during treatment. A patient noted, "The medication has a long half-life... when you stop taking it, it takes a long time to get out of your system," highlighting concerns around withdrawal.

Noteworthy

Some extreme experiences include patients who faced suicidal ideation and significant health crises after using Vraylar. One reviewer wrote, "The drug ruined my life... I lost my career, sold my house just because this med altered my mind!" Another user shared that their husband became increasingly paranoid on Vraylar and ultimately died by suicide.

Conclusion

Vraylar has given hope and significant improvement to many patients struggling with severe mental health issues. However, the potential for serious side effects, including weight gain and psychiatric crises, makes it essential for users to proceed with caution and have close medical supervision.
