Luvox CR Oral

Positive

Many patients report life-changing effects from Luvox CR, particularly in reducing OCD symptoms, anxiety, and panic attacks. One user states, "This medication seriously saved my life!!" Another mentions, "Completely annihilated my obsessive thoughts and compulsive behavior within a week." The medication has allowed some to return to regular social interactions and daily life. Cost-effectiveness is also a noted benefit, with users mentioning low monthly costs.

Negative

Common side effects include drowsiness and fatigue, leading some users to express frustration. One patient said, "I am extremely sleepy ALL the time," while another noted feeling "tired all the time." Other reported issues include withdrawal symptoms when missing doses, with one user describing "excruciating" withdrawal and another warning about potentially severe withdrawal experiences.

Noteworthy

  • A patient experienced life-threatening symptoms, including a drastic drop in blood pressure and a heart rate as low as 40, necessitating hospital care.
  • Another reported severe abdominal pain and emotional numbness, ultimately deciding to stop the medication.

Conclusion

Overall, Luvox CR appears to provide significant relief for many individuals suffering from OCD and anxiety, though side effects and withdrawal issues are notable concerns. Patients should consult closely with their doctors to manage doses and monitor side effects effectively.
