Positive

Noteworthy

One patient expressed dramatic distress, stating, "I just became suicidal... I'm MORE hopeless," indicating a severe negative reaction that may warrant further investigation into their treatment approach. This extreme sentiment contrasts sharply with those who found some level of improvement, showcasing the drug's varied effects.

Conclusion

Overall, patient reviews of Risperidone Microspheres Intramuscular present a mixed picture, with some users benefitting from mood stabilization, while many others report debilitating side effects.
