Depakene Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Depakene Oral effectively controls seizures, with one user stating, "I love that it worked for me and helped prevent any future seizures for my epilepsy." Additionally, some individuals with mood disorders found stabilization, with comments like "This medicine is easy to tolerate and it stabilized my mood as I am a hypomanic BP."

Negative

On the downside, several users experienced severe side effects. One reviewer noted "the most severe depression I've ever experienced," which worsened with increased dosage. Another reported profound changes, including "major migraines," depression, and even symptoms of psychosis: "I heard things that were not there" and "my skin crawled like something was trying to get out." Some expressed feelings of numbness and loneliness, highlighting a disconnect from personal life and family.

Noteworthy

A particularly alarming review details a child's irrational and violent behavior attributed to Depakene, including "writing stories about death" and attempting "self-harm." The user stated, "I've even attempted to run away from home... It still scares me to this day."

Conclusion

Overall, while Depakene Oral has been beneficial for seizure control and mood stabilization for some, many patients report severe and distressing side effects, leading to feelings of fear and confusion regarding the medication's impact on their mental health and quality of life. Caution and thorough monitoring are advised for those considering its use.
