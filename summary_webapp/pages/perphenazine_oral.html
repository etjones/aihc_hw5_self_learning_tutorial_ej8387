Perphenazine Oral

Positive

Many patients express that Perphenazine has been effective in managing their severe symptoms, such as hallucinations and mood swings, without the sedative effects often associated with other antipsychotics. Reviews highlight feelings of stability and clarity of thought. One patient noted, "This medication has been a life-saver for me," praising its role in alleviating insomnia and improving sleep quality. Another user shared, "It has helped me become more stable with my bipolar disorder since I have been taking it."

Negative

Side effects mentioned include drowsiness, dizziness, and irritability. A few patients reported troubling experiences such as hallucinations, severe stomach issues, and permanent impotence. One review stated the drug "made me hallucinate and disillusioned, made me extremely sick at my stomach," indicating significant distress. Another individual noted that while it worked initially, their experience deteriorated, offering a cautionary perspective about reactions to the medication.

Noteworthy

One long-term user remarked, "I have been taking this drug for over 20 years with no side effects except for a little drowsiness which I counteract with coffee." This highlights the longevity and effectiveness for some patients. Conversely, a newer user expressed frustration, stating, "So far, nothing but blurriness, dizziness, and disorientation," underscoring the varied experiences among patients.

Conclusion

Perphenazine generally garners positive feedback for its effectiveness in symptom management while posing a risk of notable side effects. It appears particularly beneficial for patients who have struggled to find a suitable antipsychotic, though individual experiences can vary greatly.
