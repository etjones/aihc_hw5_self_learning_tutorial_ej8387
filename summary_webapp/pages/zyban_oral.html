Zyban Oral

Positive

Many patients express gratitude for Zyban, highlighting its effectiveness in helping them quit smoking after years of failure with other methods. Users report dramatic life changes, such as "Zyban saved my life" and describe it as "a miracle." Several users noted the reduction in cravings and withdrawal symptoms, with one saying, "I was able to quit smoking with ease." Others found additional benefits, like increased energy and improved mood.

Negative

Some reviews reveal significant side effects, including severe depression, insomnia, and anxiety. One individual mentioned experiencing "total insomnia" and another described their life as "filled with anger" after taking Zyban. Others reported milder symptoms like dry mouth and nausea, but they still found the experience challenging.

Noteworthy

One user reported "lost complete sight in left eye," and another alarmingly stated, "I felt like I was having a heart attack." These extreme experiences stand out as particularly concerning among the reviews. Additionally, another reviewer noted a staggering increase in blood pressure, urging extreme caution in using the medication.

Conclusion

Zyban has proven effective for many in quitting smoking, often described as life-changing. However, side effects can range from mild discomfort to severe reactions, leading to mixed feelings about the drug. While many celebrate their success with Zyban, caution is advised due to the potential risks involved.
