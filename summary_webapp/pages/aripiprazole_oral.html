Conversely, several reviewers reported severe side effects, including weight gain, drowsiness, and other debilitating symptoms. Noteworthy

Conclusion

Aripiprazole seems to work well for many, offering significant emotional relief and improved social functioning. However, potential side effects, especially weight gain and neurological symptoms, are common concerns that could lead some users to seek alternative treatments. Prospective users should consult healthcare professionals to weigh the benefits against these risks.
