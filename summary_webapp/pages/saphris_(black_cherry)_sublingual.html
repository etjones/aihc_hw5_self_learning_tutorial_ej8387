Saphris (black Cherry) Sublingual

Positive

Many patients report that Saphris significantly improves their mood and sleep quality. One user noted, "This is a total gamechanger for me MDD...within 3 days I felt as close to normal as I have ever been." Another user emphasized that it allowed them to "finally control my moods" and helped them engage more actively in daily life. Several reviews praised the medication for alleviating symptoms of insomnia and enhancing overall well-being.

Negative

Common negative experiences include weight gain and difficulties with dosage administration due to its unpleasant taste. A user stated, "the taste is TERRIBLE," and others mentioned significant side effects such as dizziness, confusion, and severe allergic reactions. Several users expressed frustration over losing motivation or experiencing feelings of depersonalization and mental confusion. One user lamented, "I completely lost my appetite, couldn't hardly walk," attributing it to the medication.

Noteworthy

Outlier experiences include a user who reported eye damage after prolonged use, stating, "She couldn't believe how fast my vision was deteriorating," and another who described terrifying hallucinations and delusions. One individual experienced severe allergic reactions and highlighted the dangers of mixing alcohol with the medication, sharing, "DO NOT drink alcohol while discontinuing Saphris."

Conclusion

While many patients find Saphris beneficial for managing mood disorders and promoting sleep, the drug is not without its issues, particularly concerning side effects such as weight gain and severe allergic reactions. Individual experiences vary widely, and some report life-altering consequences, both positive and negative.
