Pamelor Oral

Positive

Many patients have reported significant relief from migraines and headaches after starting Pamelor. For example, one user mentioned, "I have not had a headache in 8 days now!" Another noted that it has been a "miracle drug for me," helping with fibromyalgia symptoms. Patients have also mentioned improvements in mood and sleep quality, stating that the medication helped them feel "happier" and more "energetic."

Negative

Common complaints include persistent side effects such as dry mouth, weight gain, and drowsiness. One patient experienced severe headaches and suicidal thoughts, highlighting the psychological impact: "I've become very irritable... I have thought about suicide three times since starting this." Another individual mentioned experiencing hallucinations and nearly severe heart problems: "Please beware when taking this medication."

Noteworthy

  • A 16-year-old girl shared her severe reaction, leading to hospitalization due to a heart condition attributed to Pamelor: "I felt pretty good for about three months... but then became completely insane for two weeks."
  • Long-term users have reported weight gain and difficulty managing it: "I've had to deal with weight gain, which is extremely difficult to work its way off."

Conclusion

Pamelor appears to be effective for many patients seeking relief from migraines, fibromyalgia, and depression, often improving mood and sleep. However, the medication can lead to significant side effects, including weight gain, drowsiness, and severe psychological reactions in some users, suggesting it may not be suitable for everyone.
