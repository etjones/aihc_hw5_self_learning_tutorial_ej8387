Haloperidol Lactate Oral

Positive

Some patients report that Haloperidol Lactate Oral has significantly improved their condition. One user noted, “Began using this drug when I was put on Hospice due to Cancer. Thus far it has given me the best results out of all the others,” highlighting its effectiveness in providing relief during a challenging time.

Negative

Contrastingly, many reviews express dissatisfaction, particularly centered around the lack of calming effects and aggravation of symptoms. One person described their experience as being "purely enraged" after administration, claiming, "I tell u what....it did not calm me down at all." Another user reported worsening symptoms, stating, “my tourettes got worse since then I have been having a lot of motor tics and screaming,” indicating a troubling response to the medication.

Noteworthy

A particularly concerning review mentioned a patient’s daughter continuing to experience hallucinations despite being on a high dosage, raising questions about the drug's efficacy for certain individuals: "my daughter's hallucinations are still present and she's on a high dosage."

Conclusion

Patients' experiences with Haloperidol Lactate vary widely; while some find it beneficial, many report negative side effects and a lack of relief from their symptoms. The distinct divide in feelings suggests the necessity for careful monitoring and individualized treatment plans.
