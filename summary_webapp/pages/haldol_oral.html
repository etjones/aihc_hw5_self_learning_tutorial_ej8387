Haldol Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Haldol is effective for managing severe symptoms of schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, and Tourette's syndrome. One patient stated, "Haldol has worked at one dose or another for a lot of years now," noting its ability to stabilize their mood. Others appreciated the drug's efficiency in reducing hallucinations and improving focus, with one individual saying it helped them think more clearly. Some users found it beneficial when used in combination with other medications.

Negative

Conversely, Haldol has garnered severe criticism for its debilitating side effects. Several patients experienced dystonia, involuntary movements, and extreme sedation, leading them to feel like "zombies." One patient expressed that Haldol made them "unable to walk correctly" and resulted in "drooling and unintelligible slurred speech." The drug is particularly concerning for elderly patients, with multiple reviews citing its link to significant cognitive decline and physical deterioration, like one user who remarked on their mother's rapid decline post-administration.

Noteworthy

A user recounted a harrowing experience: after being administered Haldol, they suffered from "extreme headaches," shortness of breath, and massive anxiety, leading to persistent negative effects lasting beyond their treatment. Another reviewer noted, "This drug nearly killed my grandmother," emphasizing the drug's potential dangers, especially in the elderly.

Conclusion

Overall, while Haldol has had positive effects for some individuals in managing severe psychological symptoms, the considerable risk of adverse effects has left many patients with a sense of distress and dissatisfaction. Caution is advised, particularly for vulnerable populations such as the elderly.
