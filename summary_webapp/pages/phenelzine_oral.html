Phenelzine Oral

Positive

Many patients express strong positive feelings about Phenelzine (Nardil), highlighting its effectiveness in treating severe depression and social anxiety. One user states, "It WORKED and wonderfully so!" noting relief not just from depression but also from anxiety. Another reviewer, who has taken the medication for over 30 years, emphasizes that it has helped him tremendously, suggesting a long-term positive impact on his quality of life. Several review mention that Nardil is more effective compared to other treatments they have tried, and many feel it has been life-changing.

Negative

Conversely, some patients report significant side effects and challenges while on Phenelzine. Issues such as nausea, weight gain, tiredness, and heightened anxiety were noted. One patient described feeling "more depression with suicidal thoughts" and experiencing postural hypotension after a dosage change. Another emphasized the severity of withdrawal symptoms after a dose reduction, warning others against rapid changes in dosage.

Noteworthy

A particularly striking negative experience was shared by a user who needed hospitalization after a dose was cut in half, stating, “Never allow your dose to be reduced as quickly!” This highlights the potential dangers associated with dosage adjustments.

Conclusion

Overall, Phenelzine appears to offer substantial benefits for many patients struggling with severe depression and anxiety, yet it can also come with challenging side effects and risks if not managed carefully. Ensuring proper dosage and monitoring appears crucial for those on this medication.
