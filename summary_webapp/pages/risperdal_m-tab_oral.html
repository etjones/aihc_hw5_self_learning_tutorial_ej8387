Risperdal M-TAB Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Risperdal M-TAB has significantly improved their quality of life, enabling them to manage symptoms that were previously overwhelming. One user noted, "Went from being uncontrollable to being able to have a manageable day. Life saver!" Another found it beneficial for panic attacks, stating it worked well for her frustration when taken occasionally. Parents reported some success in managing symptoms for their children with autism and ADHD, with one mentioning long-term usage showing positive effects.

Negative

On the downside, numerous users expressed severe concerns about the side effects associated with Risperdal M-TAB. Reports of significant weight gain are common, with one patient stating that they gained 80 pounds, while another mentioned gaining over 20 pounds despite efforts to control their weight. Some users also experienced distressing symptoms, including "zombie-like characteristics" and involuntary movements similar to Parkinson's disease. Strong criticisms include claims that the medication is "the most dangerous drug produced anywhere in the entire world."

Noteworthy

Several patients highlighted extremely negative experiences, with one claiming the drug nearly killed them by damaging their heart and causing uncontrollable movements. A parent described their son becoming aggressive and irritable after using the medication, raising concerns about long-term consequences.

Conclusion

Overall, experiences with Risperdal M-TAB vary widely among patients. While some have found substantial relief from their symptoms, many express serious concerns about weight gain and distressing side effects, leading to a lack of recommendation for others.
