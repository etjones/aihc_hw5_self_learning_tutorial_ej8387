Serax Oral

Positive

Many patients express gratitude for Serax, describing it as a "life saver" that effectively alleviates anxiety and panic attacks. Users noted improvements in daily functioning, such as the ability to teach or address groups without overwhelming fear. Notable comments include: "This medication has made my life so much better" and "I have found this the only thing that brought my life back to order." Long-term users appreciate its consistent effectiveness and minimal side effects compared to other anxiety medications.

Negative

Some users reported feelings of dependency and withdrawal-like symptoms when reducing dosages. One reviewer mentioned, "I would feel like I had a hangover if I decreased my dosage." Additionally, concerns about the drug's interaction with alcohol were highlighted, with anecdotes about unresponsiveness when taken with alcohol.

Noteworthy

One user mentioned being a heavy drinker but achieving sobriety after starting Serax, stating, "Since I started serax I haven't had a drink in 30 days... it has saved my life." Conversely, another user described the medication as ineffective when taken with alcohol, leading to a lack of responsiveness.

Conclusion

Overall, the sentiment towards Serax is overwhelmingly positive, particularly among patients who struggle with anxiety. While there are concerns about potential dependency and interactions with alcohol, many users emphasize its life-changing benefits and effective anxiety management over the long term.
