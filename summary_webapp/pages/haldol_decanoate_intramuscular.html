Haldol Decanoate Intramuscular

Positive

Patients generally report that Haldol Decanoate is effective in managing symptoms of their conditions. Many patients appreciate the reduction of delusions and hallucinations, with one user noting, “I no longer hear voices and this drug seems to be working for me.” Another review reflects contentment with the treatment efficacy: “This prescription worked very well for me.”

Negative

Several patients expressed concerns about side effects, particularly lethargy and significant weight gain. One individual stated, “Just made me gain about 60 pounds” and another described feeling “miserable” even after waking. Severe side effects were highlighted as well, such as muscle tremors and drowsiness: “lethargy somnolence muscle tremors drowsiness difficulty speaking.”

Noteworthy

A particularly concerning experience involved a family member who became severely altered by the medication: “He is always out of it and just walks around like a zombie... I almost died, my throat closed off... this medication should be administered in SMALL doses.” This reflects the potential for drastic negative impacts on a patient's quality of life.

Conclusion

Overall, while Haldol Decanoate can provide significant symptom relief for some, many users report troubling side effects that can detract from their overall experience. Balancing the medication's efficacy against side effects appears to be a common theme in the reviews.
