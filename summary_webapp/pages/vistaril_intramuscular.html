Vistaril Intramuscular

Positive

Many patients found Vistaril effective for treating anxiety and nausea. One user stated, "this treatment is awesome. I love drugs," indicating a very favorable experience. Others reported that it helped alleviate specific symptoms, such as severe itching and anxiety, with one review noting, "after starting this medication, all symptoms of itching...disappeared."

Negative

A significant number of patients reported unpleasant side effects including extreme drowsiness and nightmares. One individual mentioned, "I was afraid to take it during the day for fear that I will be a zombie," while another wrote, "I was told I could not take anything to get rid of the headaches while I was on this medication." Additionally, instances of severe headaches and adverse reactions, such as a stroke-like experience, were highlighted as alarming outcomes.

Noteworthy

A particularly concerning review described horrific side effects: "I ended up back at the hospital because I started having symptoms of a stroke. Scariest thing I have ever experienced. I will never take it again." Furthermore, one user stated that Vistaril led to a drastic alteration in mood, expressing feelings of being "jittery" and "out of it" long after use.

Conclusion

Overall, while some patients reported positive effects of Vistaril for anxiety and nausea, many expressed significant dissatisfaction due to side effects like extreme fatigue, vivid nightmares, and severe headaches. The drug seems to have a mixed reputation, with a notable number of patients experiencing severe adverse reactions.
