Vistaril IM (HCl Salt) Intramuscular

Positive

Many patients report that Vistaril IM is effective in reducing anxiety and controlling symptoms such as hives and itching. One user noted, "This medication controls this and stops the itching and rash," highlighting its ability to alleviate stress-related physical symptoms. Additionally, another patient experienced improved sleep during the initial use, stating, "I finally got some sleep on this drug for the first week."

Negative

Negative experiences often involve side effects that disrupt daily life. Some individuals reported severe headaches and nausea, with one person mentioning, "made me sick to my stomach. had to stop using it." Others experienced heightened anxiety and jitteriness, as indicated by a user who said they were "shaking really bad, too much energy & nervousness." A few reviews also indicated worsening symptoms, such as returning headaches or severe itching post-infusion.

Noteworthy

A particularly concerning account described a patient feeling as though they were "having a heart attack" after taking Vistaril for migraines, highlighting a potential severity in adverse reactions that warrants further attention.

Conclusion

Overall, Vistaril IM appears to provide relief from anxiety and certain physical symptoms for some patients, but it may come with significant side effects that can negate its benefits. Users who have severe or unusual reactions are encouraged to speak with a healthcare provider for more tailored advice.
