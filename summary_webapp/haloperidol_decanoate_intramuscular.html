Haloperidol Decanoate Intramuscular

Positive

Many patients report significant benefits from Haloperidol Decanoate, particularly in managing symptoms related to bipolar disorder and psychosis. It has been praised for helping with sleep and reducing delusional thoughts. One user stated, "I am on haloperidol for bipolar; it helps me sleep and prevents my delusional thoughts."

Negative

Conversely, others express strong dissatisfaction, especially those with Tourette's syndrome. One reviewer described their experience as detrimental, stating, "THIS. STUFF. SUCKS." They reported worsened tics, difficulties in concentrating, and negative impacts on academic performance. Another user noted that the medication does not address negative symptoms, such as "loss of motivation" and "lack of interest."

Noteworthy

One exceptionally negative review stems from a long-term user who had never struggled academically before, stating they flunked two classes while on this medication due to its cognitive side effects.

Conclusion

Overall, patient experiences with Haloperidol Decanoate vary widely. While some individuals find it beneficial for managing psychosis and mood symptoms, others report it exacerbates their condition and causes significant cognitive challenges.
