Hydroxyzine Pamoate Oral

Positive

Many patients have reported that Hydroxyzine Pamoate effectively helps with anxiety and sleep issues. Users appreciate its non-addictive nature compared to benzodiazepines. One patient said, "Within an hour, anxiety symptoms reduce or are completely relieved," while another noted, "It helps me relax my mind and body so I can fall asleep." Others highlighted its effectiveness for managing itching associated with allergies.

Negative

On the downside, numerous patients experienced adverse side effects, including excessive drowsiness, grogginess, and increased anxiety. One reviewer remarked, "It makes me feel like a zombie," while another reported severe side effects such as confusion and hallucinations: "I felt like I was crawling out of my skin." Many also mentioned that it failed to alleviate their anxiety or insomnia, with one stating, "Completely ineffective for anxiety. Might as well be taking tic-tacs."
