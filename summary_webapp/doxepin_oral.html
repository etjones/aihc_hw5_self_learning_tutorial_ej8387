Doxepin Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Doxepin has been highly effective for insomnia, helping them sleep through the night. Several users described life-changing experiences, with one saying, "It saved my life. Helped me sleep really good." Others appreciated its non-habit forming nature, with a user highlighting the absence of "groggy 'hangover' feeling" common with other medications. Some found improvements in mood and anxiety, with comments like, "this has been the miracle drug for my doctor's plan."

Negative

While many found success, numerous users experienced significant side effects. Common complaints included severe daytime drowsiness, grogginess, and weight gain. For instance, one user noted, "I have gained ten pounds in a little more than a month," while another said, "needed 12 hours of sleep. Too sleepy to drive." Some reported severe reactions, such as one who described needing to be hospitalized after experiencing abnormal heartbeats.

Noteworthy

Several reviews highlighted unusual or severe side effects. One individual detailed having vivid and frightening nightmares, resulting in a deep depression that lasted for days. Another user reported an episode involving sleepwalking and memory loss, resulting in actions that were concerning and dangerous.
