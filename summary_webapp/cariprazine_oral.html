Cariprazine Oral

Positive

Many patients report strong improvements in their mental health while using Cariprazine. One user with schizoaffective disorder stated, "this is the only drug that works for me," highlighting significant relief from symptoms like intrusive thoughts and depressive episodes, with no side effects noted. Another patient with a long history of schizophrenia and depression mentioned "no side effects" aside from increased appetite, suggesting a manageable experience overall.

Negative

However, there are mixed feelings about Cariprazine. A patient clarified that while the drug reduced manic symptoms, they still felt depressed, implying limited effectiveness for certain individuals. Another review reflects a strong negative experience, stating, “I feel much better now I have stopped this medication,” indicating that the drug exacerbated psychosis for that user.

Noteworthy

One individual shared that the medication brought life-changing benefits, contrasting sharply with experiences of others who were unable to find relief or had adverse reactions, particularly mentioning a return to improved functionality after resuming the medication.

Conclusion

Cariprazine has demonstrated remarkable efficacy for some, particularly in alleviating severe symptoms of schizophrenia and schizoaffective disorder without notable side effects. However, others have found it ineffective or counterproductive, underscoring the varied responses patients have to this medication.
