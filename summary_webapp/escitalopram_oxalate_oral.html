Escitalopram Oxalate Oral

Positive

Many patients reported significant improvements in their anxiety and depression after starting Escitalopram. Common favorable points include enhanced emotional regulation, decreased panic attacks, and improved overall mood. A user noted, “this medication has changed my life... the gloom and sadness I had struggled with for 7 years started to fade away.” Others appreciated its role in managing their anxiety effectively, saying it allowed them to engage more fully with life, such as one user who remarked, "I now do everything with them [family] and as a result lost 73 lbs."

Negative

Conversely, numerous patients experienced troubling side effects, including anxiety intensification, memory issues, and sexual dysfunction. One review cautioned that Escitalopram "caused serious permanent side effects / brain damage," while another user expressed extreme distress, stating: “this drug is a poison.” Many reported struggles with weight gain, lethargy, and emotional numbness, with one user stating, “made me lazy and stupid. gained 120lbs will on this horrible drug.”

Noteworthy

  • A user reported "bizarre thoughts" and insomnia after a short duration, leading to serious concerns about long-term effects.
  • Another patient developed severe sexual dysfunction, stating: "I have been celibate for 10 months now... this drug acts as a permanent castration agent in some percentage of its users."

Conclusion

Escitalopram elicits a diverse array of responses, showing potential as a life-changing treatment for some while concurrently posing risks of severe side effects for others. Patients express a wide range of experiences, from complete enhancement of emotional well-being to debilitating adverse reactions, indicating the importance of individualized treatment and thorough monitoring.
