Navane Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Navane has been effective for managing their mental health symptoms, particularly those with bipolar disorder and psychotic features. One user stated, "It has been the only medicine that has ever worked for me to live a life to the fullest." Another echoed, "Navane has helped me so very much, I would be a mess without it." It is noted for having fewer side effects compared to other medications, with one patient highlighting that it is "far superior to many of the newer psych medications."

Negative

Conversely, several reviews indicated significant negative symptoms associated with Navane. Common complaints include severe weight gain, tardive dyskinesia, and loss of periods, with one user mentioning "swelling of the tongue" alongside other distressing effects. An extremely negative experience described it as "HELL" and warned against its use unless absolutely necessary. Other users reported distressing involuntary movements, loss of emotion, and anxiety.
