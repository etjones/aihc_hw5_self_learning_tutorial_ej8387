Risperidone M-Tab Oral

Positive

Many patients report significant calming effects from Risperidone, stating it has helped manage anxiety and mood swings effectively. For example, one patient shared, "I was extremely anxious and worried all the time and now I'm calm and peaceful." Another noted that it has been life-changing for their child with autism, stating, "He is like a completely different kid. I'm grateful I have my boy back!" Overall, the medication seems to aid in maintaining a stable mood for many users.

Negative

On the downside, weight gain is a common concern among users, with several reports of significant increases in body weight, such as one user stating, "Triggered panic attacks, gained 35 lbs. in 3 months." Additionally, some have experienced anxiety, racing hearts, and decreased sex drive. One patient expressed mixed feelings, saying, "I do feel calmer but my energy level & attention span is lower."

Noteworthy

A particularly concerning account comes from a parent describing their daughter's rapid weight gain and significant lifestyle changes, stating, "It has changed her entire appearance, made her slow and lumbering." Another patient mentioned muscle stiffness, noting, "stiffness has set in muscles," suggesting a potential long-term side effect.

Conclusion

Overall, while Risperidone M-Tab Oral has provided substantial relief in managing mood disorders and anxiety for many, it is associated with significant side effects, particularly weight gain and emotional side effects. These drawbacks can lead to complicated feelings about the medication, especially for those who value its calming effects but struggle with physical health changes.
