Paxil CR Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Paxil CR has dramatically improved their lives, citing significant reductions in anxiety, panic attacks, and depressive symptoms. Common positive sentiments include: - "Paxil saved my life!" - "It's the best medication for my anxiety and panic disorder." - "After starting Paxil, I felt normal again." - "I can now function in public."

Negative

Conversely, the medication is associated with a range of negative experiences: - Weight gain is frequently mentioned, with some users noting increases of 30-50 pounds. - Many report sexual side effects, including diminished libido and difficulties with orgasm. - Patients also express concern about severe withdrawal symptoms, including mood swings, dizziness, and electric shock sensations upon discontinuation. - One user described the weaning process as “the worst experience of my life,” highlighting the complexities of coming off the drug.

Noteworthy

  • Some reviews include extreme reactions: one patient reported they experienced severe suicidal thoughts after starting Paxil, while another described a neurological reaction leading to hospitalization.
  • A recurring theme is the contrast in experiences. While some found relief, others reported exacerbated symptoms or troubling side effects, such as increased anxiety or mood changes.

Conclusion

Paxil CR appears to be a double-edged sword; it can provide life-saving benefits for anxiety and depression but comes with significant risks, including serious side effects and withdrawal challenges. Patients are advised to monitor their responses closely and consult their doctors about any concerns.
