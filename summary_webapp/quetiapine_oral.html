Quetiapine Oral

Positive

Many patients reported that Quetiapine effectively helped with mood stabilization, improved sleep quality, and reduced symptoms of mania and anxiety. A user noted, "This medication saved my life!" and another stated, "It allowed me to feel normal and sleep normal." Some patients praised its effectiveness in managing bipolar disorder and PTSD symptoms, such as having significantly fewer panic attacks and improved control over mood swings.

Negative

Conversely, numerous adverse effects were highlighted, particularly weight gain, sedation, and dependency. One user lamented, "Over the last 5 years, I have gained 150 lbs." Others expressed feelings of lethargy, brain fog, and a "zombie" state. Strong negative sentiments were shared by a patient stating, "It made me suicidal and felt like I was buried underwater with it." There were also alarming reports of more severe complications, such as seizures and hospitalizations after taking the drug.

Noteworthy

Several experiences stood out as particularly distressing, including a patient who wrote, "My sister was prescribed this medication... committed suicide weeks later." Another user mentioned severe reactions leading them to intensive care, saying they felt suicidal after just a few days on the medication.

Conclusion

Quetiapine appears to have a mixed reputation among patients, providing significant help for some while causing severe complications for others. The consensus suggests that while it can be beneficial for managing certain mental health conditions, the side effects and risks, particularly concerning weight gain and emotional numbness, pose serious concerns. It is crucial for patients to consult healthcare providers closely when considering its use.
