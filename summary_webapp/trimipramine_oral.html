Trimipramine Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Trimipramine effectively alleviates symptoms of depression and anxiety, helping them feel significantly better. Users appreciate its calming effects and ability to improve overall mood, with one stating it "helps the depression/anxiety" they have been struggling with.

Negative

Common complaints include side effects like weight gain, constipation, and increased cravings for nicotine. Some users express concern over these side effects affecting their quality of life. One individual specifically mentioned starting to smoke again due to cravings raised by the medication.

Noteworthy

Notably, no outlier experiences with severe adverse effects or unique circumstances were reported. Most reviews focus on the common side effects and benefits of the medication.

Conclusion

Overall, patients have mixed feelings about Trimipramine. While it is generally effective in treating depression and anxiety, the side effects such as weight gain and other discomforts significantly impact users' experiences.
