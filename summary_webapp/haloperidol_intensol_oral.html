Positive

Noteworthy

Conclusion

Overall, while many patients found Haloperidol Intensol life-changing in managing severe symptoms, a notable fraction experienced uncomfortable and unsettling side effects. The drug has the potential for significant positive impact, but individual experiences vary widely.
