Abilify Maintena Intramuscular

Positive

Many patients praise Abilify Maintena for its effectiveness in managing symptoms of their mental health conditions. One reviewer described it as a “Miracle Drug,” noting that they experienced zero side effects and no long-term health issues while on it. Another user mentioned that it helped them control auditory hallucinations, improving their overall experience with the medication.

Negative

Conversely, a significant number of users reported severe side effects, particularly weight gain and gastrointestinal issues. Multiple reviewers noted drastic weight increases, with one stating they gained 100 lbs and felt forced to continue the medication despite their misery. Others experienced constipation and urinary difficulties, calling the medication "poison." Another noted hallucinations and delusions as a result of taking the drug, expressing frustration with their doctor for prescribing it.

Noteworthy

One patient highlighted the drastic negative shift in health after switching to Aristada from Abilify Maintena, leading to multiple ER visits due to severe side effects, suggesting that Abilify was significantly better for their health.

Conclusion

Overall, Abilify Maintena elicited mixed reactions among users. While some experienced remarkable benefits in symptom management, a considerable number struggled with severe side effects, especially concerning weight gain and other physical health issues.
