Endep Oral

Positive

Many patients reported significant relief from pain and improved sleep quality while using Endep. Users expressed appreciation for the medication's effectiveness in managing chronic conditions like fibromyalgia, neuropathy, and insomnia. Highlights include: - "Not only has it helped with Fibromyalgia but it also helped with my migraines and I sleep better." - "Endep has proved to be an invaluable treatment option... my life is now one that I am happy to LIVE." - "pain almost gone sleep better overall feel great."

Negative

There are numerous complaints regarding side effects, with several users experiencing severe adverse reactions. Common issues mentioned include: - Drowsiness and brain fog, making daily tasks difficult: "I get so drowsy I can't see straight." - Weight gain, edema, and other physical side effects: "Started sweating more recently, so not sure if Endep is working anymore." - Emotional disturbances, including increased anxiety and depressive episodes: "After 3-4 days deep deep depression... suffering major pain in my head."

Noteworthy

A review mentioned a drastic decline in well-being after stopping the medication: "WARNING! it was a nightmare getting off it... reached a suicidal state." This highlights the potential risks of withdrawal from Endep.

Conclusion

Overall, Endep has a mixed reception among patients. While many appreciate its pain-relieving and sleep-enhancing properties, significant side effects and negative emotional impacts raise concerns. The variability in individual responses suggests that monitoring and consultation with a healthcare provider are essential for safe use.
