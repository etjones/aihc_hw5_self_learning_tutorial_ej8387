Imipramine Pamoate Oral

Positive

Many patients report significant relief from symptoms due to Imipramine Pamoate. For instance, one user mentioned, “I have been on it most of my life,” noting its effectiveness for psychiatric issues, stating that “it worked the best for me.” Another individual found marked improvement in incontinence after a week of use and expressed being “thrilled.” Several users appreciated the medication's capacity to alleviate severe headaches and migraines, with one mentioning, “After about a week I had my first full day in 6 months without getting any headaches.”

Negative

Conversely, many users experienced undesirable side effects. Common complaints include severe stomach pain, headaches, and fatigue. One review highlighted life-altering side effects: “THIS MED WAS SUPPOSED TO HELP MY SEVERE HEADACHES, ALL IT HAS DONE IS MAKE ME SHAKE.” Others reported cognitive dysfunction, such as memory issues, and severe agitation, stating that it worsened their conditions rather than improving them and made everyday functioning difficult.

Noteworthy

A user claimed to experience hallucinations, “seeing fleas all over our house and getting bites,” a rare and concerning side effect. Another reported extreme cognitive decline and fatigue, saying that “my muscles felt like I had the flu,” making it hard to perform daily tasks.

Conclusion

Imipramine Pamoate has garnered mixed reviews, with significant positive feedback for its efficacy in treating certain symptoms countered by a range of troubling side effects. While it offers relief for some, others find the side effects untenable, leading to a spectrum of patient experiences.
