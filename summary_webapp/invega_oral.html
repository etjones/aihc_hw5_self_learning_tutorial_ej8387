Invega Oral

Positive

Many patients reported significant improvements in their mental health after starting Invega. Key benefits include a reduction in paranoia, hallucinations, and mood swings. A user shared, "Invega cleared up my mind," and another noted, "It has completely eliminated hallucinations and paranoia in my teenage daughter." The once-a-month injection was also praised for its convenience. Patients appreciated feeling more like themselves again and mentioned increased levels of happiness and clarity in thinking.

Negative

Common side effects included significant weight gain, lethargy, and sexual dysfunction, causing frustration among many users. Some expressed strong dissatisfaction, with one saying, "Stay away from this drug; it caused me massive weight gain." Others complained of severe side effects like high prolactin levels leading to lactation, anxiety, and debilitating mental fog. Certain reviews highlighted drastic responses, including one user stating, "My brother was given this shot... wound up in ICU."

Noteworthy

A few outlier experiences were particularly alarming, with one review stating, "This medication gave me two strokes." Another user experienced severe paranoia while taking it, stating, "I couldn’t drive because I was scared I’d die."

Conclusion

User experiences with Invega varied widely, with many finding it life-changing in managing mental health issues, while a substantial number reported troubling side effects, particularly weight gain and sexual dysfunction. The overall sentiment suggests that while the medication can be beneficial, some patients may face serious adverse reactions that overshadow its advantages.
