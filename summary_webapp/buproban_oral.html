Buproban Oral

Positive

Many patients reported significant success with Buproban, often highlighting its effectiveness in reducing cravings and aiding in smoking cessation. One user stated, "This saved my life! ... I am approaching 2 years of non-smoking." Others noted that it provides energy and helps them return to normal social interactions, with one review mentioning, "I feel like the old me is back." Overall, users appreciated that it assists in quitting smoking, albeit with the need for personal motivation.

Negative

Conversely, several users experienced adverse effects or felt that the medication was ineffective. A user lamented, "DOES NOT WORK!!!!!!!!", while another reported severe side effects like extreme dizziness and anxiety, stating, "Horrible feeling, I recommend other methods of smoke cessation." A particularly alarming review detailed a severe allergic reaction resulting in a psychotic episode.

Noteworthy

One striking outlier described a life-altering experience, claiming, "Because of this drug my life was in shambles... Please don't take this drug." This contrasts sharply with other users who found it life-saving.

Conclusion

In summary, experiences with Buproban Oral vary considerably. While many found it a helpful aid in quitting smoking and improving their mental state, others faced intense negative side effects or ineffective results. Overall, personal factors like stress levels and commitment appear crucial to the medication's effectiveness.
