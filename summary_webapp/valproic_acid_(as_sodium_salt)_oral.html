Valproic Acid (as Sodium Salt) Oral

Positive

Many patients report significant benefits from taking Valproic Acid, especially in controlling seizures and stabilizing mood. One user stated, "I have been on this medication for 14 years and find it a great mood stabilizer," noting improvements in chronic headaches and overall stability. Another individual highlighted being seizure-free for over a year, attributing success to the medication combined with a healthy lifestyle.

Negative

Common side effects include stomach issues such as cramps and nausea, with some users questioning the effectiveness due to concurrent medications. One review mentioned, "this drug causes stomach problems," while another emphasized drowsiness, blurred vision, and confusion as concerns, noting, “I do experience... an inability to turn thoughts into actions as efficiently as I used to.”

Noteworthy

A patient shared a profound experience, stating, "had one full seizure waking up in hospital...NO further problems since starting VS 3 months ago," emphasizing the drug's impact on their health after a severe event.

Conclusion

Valproic Acid appears to be effective for many in managing seizures and mood stabilization, although gastrointestinal side effects are commonly reported. Patients advise consulting with healthcare providers to maximize benefits while managing side effects effectively.
