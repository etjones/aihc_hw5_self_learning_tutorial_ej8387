Limbitrol DS Oral

Positive

Many patients express strong satisfaction with Limbitrol DS, noting its effectiveness in managing symptoms. One user mentioned, "it has definitely helped me," and referred to it as "wonder drug" for alleviating panic attacks and restoring their ability to care for themselves and their child. Negative

Conversely, some patients reported adverse experiences, particularly concerning mood disturbances. One individual shared that after a week of use, "my depression got so bad for no reason, I was having nightmares and horrible thoughts." This led them to discontinue the medication in favor of an organic diet and exercise, expressing significant improvements after making that change.

Noteworthy

A standout review expressed confusion over the medication's limited prescription, stating, "I have never heard of another doctor prescribe it," despite their profoundly positive experience.

Conclusion

Overall, while many users find Limbitrol DS effective and life-changing for conditions like anxiety and sleep disturbances, some experience significant negative effects on their mood and mental state. The mixed feedback underscores the importance of monitoring patients closely when starting this medication.
