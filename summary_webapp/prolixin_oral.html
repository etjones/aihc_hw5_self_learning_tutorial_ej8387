Prolixin Oral

Positive

Many patients reported significant improvements in their mental health after taking Prolixin. One reviewer noted, "I have my Spouse... back!!!" within just 24 hours of taking the medication for severe psychosis, highlighting rapid and transformative effects. Others expressed relief in anxiety symptoms, with one reviewer stating, "I almost immediately felt my anxiety ease... I'm getting up easier and feel more focused."

Negative

Several patients described severe adverse effects, including uncontrollable shaking and feeling sick. One individual stated, "the medication has made me shake uncontrollable and I'm sick to my stomach all the time." A parent reported their daughter developing Neuroleptic Malignant Syndrome, resulting in a nightmarish experience in the ICU. Others had muscle cramping and discomfort, with one user listing it as an allergy due to severe side effects.

Noteworthy

This review caught attention: "Prolixin is a potent brain poison used to torture people... it is the pivot point of a debauched charlatanistic quackery." Such extreme sentiments underscore the severity of negative experiences connected to this medication.

Conclusion

Prolixin elicits a mixed response; while some patients experience life-changing benefits, many report severe and alarming side effects. The contrast in experiences suggests that while it may work well for some, it poses significant risks for others, highlighting the necessity for careful monitoring and individualized treatment plans.
