Fluphenazine Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Fluphenazine has helped stabilize their mental health, with some describing it as a "miracle drug." One user mentioned, "After continued use, I was able to lead a normal life." Others praised its effectiveness in managing symptoms of schizophrenia, stating, "It works great. No side effects." Overall, the medication's ability to assist with mood stabilization and helping patients sleep is frequently noted positively.

Negative

Conversely, various patients experienced significant side effects. Common complaints include severe muscle stiffness, restlessness, and other involuntary movements. One review highlighted a devastating reaction: "I ended up with severe full body muscular contractions and vomiting," requiring emergency intervention. Other drawbacks include nightmares and heightened anxiety after taking the medication, as expressed by multiple reviewers who reported unusual sleep disturbances.

Noteworthy

An alarming experience involves a patient suffering from acute dystonia, resulting in extreme muscle spasms and rigidity: "I couldn't swallow... I drooled and vomited uncontrollably." This reaction necessitated emergency treatment. Another patient expressed feeling as if they "were going to die" due to muscle stiffness and sleep talking.

Conclusion

The experiences with Fluphenazine are polarizing. Many find it life-changing, while others endure unacceptable side effects. This highlights the importance of monitoring and adjusting treatment as necessary, considering both the benefits and potential risks associated with the medication.
