Paliperidone Oral

Positive

Many patients reported significant improvements in their mental health with Paliperidone, especially for conditions like schizophrenia, bipolar disorder, and schizoaffective disorder. Some mentioned feeling better than ever before with positive mood regulation and reduced aggression: "This medicine has been the only medicine… a godsend!! It allows him to think clear feel better and live his life every day in a good mood." Others noted better mood management and reduced suicidal thoughts: "My mood and voices are now under control."

Negative

A recurring theme in reviews is the negative impact on energy levels and motivation. Many reported feeling lethargic and experiencing significant weight gain, with one user mentioning "loss of all my creativeness, my motivation, and my drive." Side effects were commonly highlighted, including insomnia, dry mouth, and muscle fatigue. One user expressed the severity of their negative experience: "This medication has taken my life away… I have developed insomnia and have no energy."

Noteworthy

A particularly severe review detailed serious physical issues caused by injections, leading to lasting muscle pain: “I am being tortured; I will never recover.” This highlights the potential for extreme negative experiences associated with the medication.

Conclusion

While Paliperidone has helped many patients find relief from severe mental health symptoms, the feedback is mixed, with significant reports of side effects that detracted from the overall experience. Responses range from deep gratitude for life-changing benefits to frustration over debilitating symptoms and diminished quality of life.
