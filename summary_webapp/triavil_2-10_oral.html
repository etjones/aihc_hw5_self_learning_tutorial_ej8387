Triavil 2-10 Oral

Positive

Many patients report positive experiences with Triavil, particularly in terms of alleviating symptoms of depression and anxiety. One user shared, "After a couple pills I felt so much better, I gained my appetite back and felt relaxed and well." Another stated, "When I took Triavil in three days I felt at ease, happy, and went to work." Overall, these individuals appreciated the medication's effectiveness in improving their mood and quality of life.

Negative

On the downside, several patients mentioned unpleasant withdrawal symptoms when trying to discontinue the medication. One user recounted their struggle with withdrawal, stating, "I went through agony when withdrawing," which included itching and anxiety. Others expressed dissatisfaction with the medication's tendency to induce sedation or a feeling of being "zoned out."

Noteworthy

A particularly strong negative account highlighted a patient's experience with dosage increases: "I realized I was becoming zoned out" and mentioned that they were pushed to take up to six pills a night. This raises concerns about the dosing practices utilized by some prescribers.

Conclusion

Overall, while many patients found Triavil beneficial for managing depression and anxiety, there are significant concerns about withdrawal symptoms and the risks associated with dosage management. These mixed feelings illustrate the need for careful monitoring and support from healthcare providers when using this medication.
