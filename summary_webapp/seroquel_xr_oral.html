Seroquel XR Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Seroquel XR has significantly improved their quality of life, particularly in terms of mood stability, insomnia, and anxiety reduction. For instance, one user states, "This drug has changed my life... I am alive and enjoying a good life free of anxiety, insomnia, and horrible depression." Several reviews emphasize the medication's efficacy in reducing manic episodes and improving sleep quality, with comments like "It helps me sleep and stops the voices." Others appreciate the medication for making them feel more engaged in life, with one user stating they "could finally sleep" and began enjoying life again.

Negative

Weight gain is a frequently mentioned downside, with many users reporting substantial increases in weight after beginning treatment. A common sentiment is captured in the phrase, "I have gained over 35 lbs," which many express alongside feelings of frustration and depression about their changed bodies. Other noted side effects include fatigue, grogginess, and cognitive issues. Various patients fear potential long-term health complications, such as diabetes or severe withdrawal symptoms if attempting to discontinue the medication. One individual warned: "Only take this drug if you're terminally ill!" alongside accounts of severe experiences like hallucinations and memory loss.

Noteworthy

Some reviews highlight particularly severe reactions, such as a patient who reported "feeling catatonic" after a dose, or another who faced "severe hair loss" and pronounced difficulties in daily tasks, contributing to a perception of "being a prisoner in my own body." The mental fog was a common theme, alongside mentions of intense cravings for unhealthy food and lethargy.

Conclusion

Seroquel XR induces a mixed response among users, effectively stabilizing moods and aiding sleep for many, while simultaneously causing notable weight gain and sedation. The trade-off between mental health benefits and physical health concerns creates a nuanced narrative, with several patients advocating for more careful monitoring of dosages and side effects. While it has been life-changing for some, the medication's propensity for adverse effects leaves others searching for alternatives.
