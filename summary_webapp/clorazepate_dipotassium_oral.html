Clorazepate Dipotassium Oral

Positive

Many patients reported significant improvements in their mental health while taking Clorazepate Dipotassium. Common praises included its effectiveness in calming anxiety, with one user stating, "It has calmed me down, relaxed me, taken the edge off anxiousness." Others highlighted its role in managing seizures, with a long-term user claiming, "There is no hint of a seizure." Many feel it allows them to function normally and cope with daily stressors. For instance, one user remarked, "I feel 'normal' for the first time in years."

Negative

Conversely, some users experienced troubling side effects and expressed dissatisfaction. Complaints included feelings of depression, dizziness, and addiction anxiety. One individual warned, "CAUTION - THIS MEDICATION IS HIGHLY ADDICTIVE," recounting severe withdrawal symptoms. Another mentioned, "I still have ANXIE BAD" despite taking the medication as prescribed, indicating it may not work for everyone.

Noteworthy

One outlier report indicated a severe reaction: "I have had hallucinations with this medicine when I took 7.5mg." This stark contrast to positive experiences underscores the variability of reactions to Clorazepate.

Conclusion

Overall, Clorazepate Dipotassium appears to offer profound benefits to many, particularly in managing anxiety and seizure activity. However, notable adverse effects and concerns of addiction raise important considerations, suggesting that patient experiences can vary significantly.
