Thorazine Oral

Positive

Many patients found Thorazine to be effective in managing symptoms of conditions like PTSD, bipolar disorder, and even nausea. Positive experiences include significant improvements in sleep quality, reduction in anxiety, and stabilization of manic symptoms. One user states, "It is a wonder drug for me... I have hope!" Another mentions, "Thorazine has helped curb my incessant thinking, stopped bizarre thoughts and probably saved my life."

Negative

Common negative experiences included feelings of fatigue and drowsiness, described as being "overmedicated" or "always tired." Some patients experienced severe side effects, with one stating, "it made the hallucinations so bad, that I actually blanked out!" Another reported, "I went from being underweight to extreme overweight" after switching medications.

Noteworthy

A patient shared a severe experience: "I woke up two days later on life support..." indicating a life-threatening reaction to the medication. Conversely, another user shared a remarkable success story: "This drug literally saved my life," highlighting its effective use for severe nausea and in improving overall well-being.

Conclusion

Thorazine appears to have a mixed reception among patients, with many noting its effectiveness in psychiatric and gastrointestinal symptoms, while concerns about side effects and extreme reactions can lead to negative experiences. Individual responses vary greatly, suggesting that medical supervision is crucial when using this medication.
