Triavil 2-25 Oral

Positive

Many patients report significant improvements in their mood and anxiety levels while using Triavil. One user stated, "Without my med...I'm home, just waiting to die. With it, I can get on a plane and fly to Chicago." Others appreciate its effectiveness for sleep, with one reviewer noting, "I sleep very well with no side effects the next day." A long-term user highlighted its consistency, saying, "I've tried other antidepressants but always come back to Triavil."

Negative

Weight gain is a commonly expressed concern, with one patient noting a drastic increase of "40 lbs". Others found that the medication made them excessively sleepy, with some dissatisfaction expressed over side effects that outweighed benefits, such as in the review: "Worked fairly well, but the side effects.....just wasn't worth it for me."

Noteworthy

An unusual report mentioned severe memory issues associated with other medications, emphasizing the surprise and relief when switching to Triavil, "I had no memory!...so this drug works great for me!"

Conclusion

Overall, Triavil is regarded as effective in managing depression and anxiety, particularly noted for its ability to improve sleep. However, concerns about weight gain and sedation are prevalent among users. Those with severe experiences highlight varying responses to the medication, which may help others in their decision-making process.
