Fluoxetine Oral

Positive

Many patients express life-changing benefits from Fluoxetine (Prozac), indicating significant improvements in their mood, anxiety levels, and overall functionality. They report feeling more in control of their emotions and experiencing increased motivation. One user stated, “Fluoxetine has turned my life around completely... It is safe to say this drug saved my life.” Others noted improvements in social interactions and daily family life, attributing these changes to the medication.

Negative

While some patients have had positive experiences, many report severe side effects including weight gain, sexual dysfunction, insomnia, and increased anxiety. A significant number mention that the medication initially worked well but later became less effective or even worsened their symptoms. A user lamented, “It made my depression much worse...” Others suffered physical side effects, with one saying, “I have constant headaches, dizziness & ringing in my ears.”

Noteworthy

Some extreme adverse reactions are also highlighted, including one individual experiencing "severe anxiety" that led to panic attacks and another who felt “horrible” after just one dose. Another user reported how the medication drastically affected their life, leading to suicidal thoughts during the initial period of use. "Thanks for the suicide thoughts and voices in my head," noted one frustrated reviewer.

Conclusion

Fluoxetine has a diverse range of effects, with a split experience among users. Many find it transformative, while others face challenging side effects or insufficient results, highlighting the importance of individualized medical care and ongoing monitoring. It serves as a reminder of the variability of responses to psychiatric medications.
