Lorazepam Intensol Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Lorazepam Intensol has significantly improved their quality of life, particularly in managing anxiety. Users express that the medication has allowed them to cope better with stressors, stating things like, "SINCE TAKING THIS MEDICATION MY ANXIETY HAS CALMED QUITE A BIT AND I AM MORE ABLE TO COPE." Others have noticed improved sleep patterns, with one user mentioning they "SLEEP FOR A FULL 8 HOURS" now. Weight loss and increased focus on personal health have also been highlighted positively.

Negative

Despite the benefits, some users have reported adverse effects. One review indicates that a young child experienced coordination issues after taking the medication, raising concerns about side effects in pediatric patients. Another user expressed severe anxiety upon starting the medication, stating, "I HAVE EXTREME ANXIETY SINCE I HAVE BEEN TAKING THIS MEDICINE MOST OF IT HAS WENT AWAY," implying a possible initial worsening of anxiety before improvement.

Noteworthy

A particularly concerning experience came from a review about a 3.5-year-old child, whose coordination was affected while on the medication. The reviewer questioned if such symptoms were common, highlighting the potential for unexpected side effects in younger patients.

Conclusion

Lorazepam Intensol appears to be effective for many in managing anxiety and improving sleep, but there are significant concerns regarding side effects, particularly in children. Some patients experienced a transition period of increased anxiety, and careful monitoring may be necessary for younger users.
