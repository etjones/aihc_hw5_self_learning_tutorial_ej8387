Paroxetine Mesylate Oral

Positive

Many patients report significant benefits from Paroxetine Mesylate, particularly in managing anxiety and panic attacks. One user shared, “This med did work well for me with my panic attacks and social anxiety... I can talk one on one with a stranger.” Another long-term user noted, “It worked on day one for me - noticed an immediate change.” Overall, patients appreciate its effectiveness in reducing anxiety symptoms.

Negative

Several patients have voiced concerns about side effects. Weight gain is a common complaint, with one individual stating, “I did experience weight gain...kind of depressing.” Sexual side effects, including reduced libido and delayed ejaculation, were also frequently mentioned, leading some to consider switching medications. One user simply remarked, “All this medicine does for me is makes me tired.”

Noteworthy

A user stated, “this treatment is awesome. I love drugs," which highlights a very enthusiastic experience, though it contrasts with the more muted responses from others.

Conclusion

Paroxetine Mesylate has shown potential in alleviating anxiety and panic symptoms for many, though it is not without its drawbacks, particularly regarding weight gain and sexual side effects. Individual experiences vary widely, emphasizing the importance of personalized treatment approaches.
