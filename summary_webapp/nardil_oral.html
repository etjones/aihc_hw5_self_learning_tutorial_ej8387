Nardil Oral

Positive

Patients overwhelmingly express gratitude and relief for Nardil, often stating it has dramatically transformed their lives. Many users highlight its effectiveness for treatment-resistant depression and anxiety, with one stating, "Nardil gave me the ability to navigate social interactions without the constant worry of being judged." Several reviews describe it as a "miracle drug" and a "life saver," insisting they have regained happiness and normalcy after years of suffering. Long-term users emphasize the importance of staying on Nardil, often stating they return to it after trying other medications in vain.

Negative

On the downside, many users report significant side effects, such as weight gain, fatigue, and insomnia. A few express concern over dietary restrictions due to the potential for serious food interactions. One user mentioned severe side effects leading them to panic and seizures upon withdrawal, highlighting the medication's risks. Other users experienced manic episodes, with one saying they became "hard to be around with friends and family."

Noteworthy

  • A user reported extreme weight gain and dangerous episodes related to blood pressure, stating, "I gained 90 pounds...I would fall asleep when driving."
  • Another review noted envoking manic behavior, leading to hospitalization: "I became manic and aggressive...had to be hospitalized to withdraw."

Conclusion

While Nardil has provided life-changing relief for many suffering from severe depression and anxiety, it does come with potential for serious side effects and dietary restrictions. Users emphasize the need for careful management and awareness of its interactions with food and other medications. Overall, it remains a vital option for those who have not found success with other treatments.
