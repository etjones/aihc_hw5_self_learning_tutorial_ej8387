Saphris Sublingual

Positive

Many patients report life-changing results with Saphris, specifically in managing symptoms of bipolar disorder and schizophrenia. A user shared, “This drug has kept me sane for 18 months. I sleep well and like the Black Cherry flavor.” Others noted improved mood stability and decreased anxiety, with one saying, “It has changed my life… I can concentrate on therapy and having a life!” Several users praised its efficacy for sleep, with one noting, “I can get out of bed in the morning, stay awake all day and fall asleep in 15-30 min.”

Negative

The most widespread complaint involves the awful taste and mouth numbness, with many likening it to “tasting gasoline” or “sucking on codeine cough syrup.” Patients also experience various significant side effects like restless legs, overwhelming drowsiness, and significant weight gain. One user described, “I gained 30lbs in 3 months,” while another said they felt “irritable and angry” on the medication. Severe reactions like hallucinations, seizures, and feelings of self-harm were recorded, contributing to some patients' decisions to discontinue use.

Noteworthy

  • A user reported severe side effects, stating, “It made me feel worse; dizzy and very tired...did not like it at all.”
  • Tragically, one review noted that Saphris "stopped my heart," emphasizing urgent caution for potential severe reactions.

Conclusion

While many patients have found Saphris effective for stabilizing mood and improving sleep, numerous accounts highlight severe negative reactions, especially regarding taste and side effects. Individual experiences vary significantly, calling for careful monitoring by healthcare professionals when initiating treatment.
