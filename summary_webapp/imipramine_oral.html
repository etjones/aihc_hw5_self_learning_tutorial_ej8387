Imipramine Oral

Positive

Many patients report imipramine as a life-changing medication. Users highlight its effectiveness for managing depression and panic disorders, often stating it dramatically reduces anxiety attacks and improves overall mood. One patient noted, “This drug was a life saver for me,” while another expressed, “After months of trying medications that didn’t work, imipramine turned my life around and I credit this med for saving my life.” Additionally, parents found it beneficial for bedwetting, with one stating their child had only 5-6 wet nights in the past year after starting the medication.

Negative

However, side effects are a common concern. Many experienced severe symptoms, including hallucinations, dizziness, and sleep disturbances. A patient described serious side effects, stating, “this drug made me lose 12 weeks of my life,” while another reported life-altering insomnia and disorientation. Others noted issues like rapid heart rate, significant constipation, and debilitating stomach pain, with one user sharing, “Both times I took this drug I eventually had to go to the hospital for severe abdominal pain.”

Noteworthy

Some users report extreme experiences, such as one individual who faced debilitating hallucinations nightly, saying, “I saw about 20 or more people in the room.” Contrastingly, another patient found miraculous relief from chronic digestive pain, stating, “I went to bed and the next day my epigastric pain was gone.”

Conclusion

Imipramine has delivered substantial benefits for many in managing emotional and physical health issues. Nevertheless, its side effects can be severe and potentially dangerous, prompting some to discontinue use or seek alternative treatments. Users recommend careful monitoring and open communication with healthcare providers when considering this medication.
