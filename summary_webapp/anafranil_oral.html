A standout experience included a patient who developed involuntary tics but felt it was worth it due to alleviated violent thoughts: "The violent thoughts that were destroying me are gone." Conversely, one patient described intense agitation and aggression while on the drug, leading to serious behavioral changes, earning it the nickname "The Devil Drug."

Conclusion

Anafranil appears to offer substantial relief for many dealing with severe OCD and anxiety, but side effects can be severe and distressing. Individual experiences vary greatly, suggesting careful management and consultation with a healthcare provider are essential when considering this medication.
