Chlordiazepoxide Oral

Positive

Many patients report that Chlordiazepoxide is effective in managing anxiety and panic attacks. Users appreciate its calming effects, often noting significant relief from anxiety symptoms and feelings of panic. One satisfied patient mentioned, "i love this med. it has helped me out-a-lot...found this one and its great." Another user stated, "just seems to calm down my inside...would recommend it for severe anxiety." Several reviews highlight its usefulness in aiding with alcohol withdrawal and tremors.

Negative

Conversely, some patients express concerns about the medication's addictive potential and side effects. One user shared that it "has made my mind numb," which was distressing for them. Another individual reported that they needed to increase their dosage to see results, raising concerns about liver enzymes. A common niggle is the slow onset of relief; for instance, a long-term user said, "it takes too long to get into the system."

Noteworthy

One patient had a particularly alarming experience, highlighting, "my liver enzymes are 1002," hinting at severe health issues. This emphasizes the need for regular monitoring when using the medication.

Conclusion

Overall, while Chlordiazepoxide is praised for its efficacy in treating anxiety and alcohol withdrawal, concerns regarding addiction and potential side effects are prevalent. Users seeking relief should weigh the benefits against these risks and consider regular medical supervision.
