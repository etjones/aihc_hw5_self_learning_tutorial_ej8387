Perphenazine-amitriptyline Oral

Positive

Many patients report life-changing effects from Perphenazine-amitriptyline, particularly for anxiety, panic disorders, and sleep issues. Users appreciated its ability to help them feel "normal" again and regain control over their emotions. One patient noted, "Became my old self again. Now have complete control of emotions." Another mentioned, "This worked for me immediately. Changed my life." Some also found it effective for migraines and fibromyalgia pain, despite it being an older medication.

Negative

Common side effects mentioned include weight gain, drowsiness, blurred vision, and dry mouth. Some patients experienced severe side effects leading to negative experiences. For instance, one user claimed it caused "super blurry vision" and described their experience as "poison," stating, "I am lucky I survived this trauma." Another complained about ongoing hallucinations and sleep issues, stating, "Just been taking this medicine a week and still hear voices."

Noteworthy

A particularly severe account detailed how the drug caused aggressive side effects, including milk production and cognitive impairment after being prescribed at a dangerously high dosage. The user condemned the practice and claimed significant emotional and cognitive damage ensued from the medication.

Conclusion

Overall, Perphenazine-amitriptyline is viewed as a powerful medication with significant benefits for many patients, especially in managing anxiety and sleep disorders. However, the potential for serious side effects and insufficient patient education may lead to negative experiences for some, underscoring the importance of careful medical oversight when prescribing this medication.
